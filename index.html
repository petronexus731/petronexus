About Petronexus

Petronexus is a professional earth science service provider led by experts in geology, petrology, thin-section microscopy, and subsurface exploration.

We specialize in coordinating high-quality investigations — from detailed petrographic analysis to comprehensive geotechnical and hydrological assessments — through strategic partnerships with accredited labs and specialists.

Our role ensures every project receives clear scientific oversight, accurate data interpretation, and actionable recommendations that support safe and efficient engineering decisions.

Our Services

We provide expert coordination and delivery of essential earth material investigations:

  • Petrographic / Petrological Analysis
    In-depth microscopic examination of rock and soil samples to determine mineral composition, texture, weathering characteristics, durability, and engineering suitability.
  • Soil & Geotechnical Testing
    Comprehensive services including borehole drilling, sampling, in-situ tests, laboratory analysis (classification, strength, compaction), and foundation recommendations.
  • Hydrological Surveys
    Groundwater assessment, aquifer mapping, infiltration studies, and hydrological data interpretation for water resource and environmental projects.
  • Drone & Aerial Surveys
    Topographic mapping, site reconnaissance, and photogrammetry using drone technology for efficient large-scale or remote assessments.
  • Petronexus Edu – Training & Knowledge Sharing
    Specialized sessions and resources on geology, petrography, and subsurface techniques — tailored for educational institutions, professionals, and organizations.
